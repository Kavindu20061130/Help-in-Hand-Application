Charity Guide: Handling Donations

Follow these steps immediately after you receive a donation notification to ensure a smooth pickup process.

1

Check Your Notification SMS

When a donor submits materials, you will receive an alert to your Phone via SMS . Review the item details carefully to ensure you can use them.

New Donation Alert #4921

Item Type: Used School Textbooks (Grade 6-9)
Quantity: Approx. 25 Books
Condition: Good / Reusable
Submitted: Jan 16, 2026 - 10:30 AM
2

Contact the Donor

Do not go to the location immediately. Call the donor first to thank them and confirm they are available.

Donor Name: Mrs. Amara Perera
Phone: 077-123-4567
Availability: Weekends after 2:00 PM
3

Pick Up the Donation

Go to the address provided in the notification. Please wear your Charity ID badge so the donor recognizes you.

Address: No. 45/B, Lotus Road, Colombo 03
Google Maps: View Location
4

Mark as "Collected "

Once you have the items, log back into the system and click the "Mark as Received" button. This updates our database and sends a final Thank You email to the donor.
